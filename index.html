|SOUND OF SPACE|

is an initiative led by Composer/Architect Emma-Kate Matthews, Architect Prof. Jane Burry, and Architect Mark Burry
Our aim is to convene a diverse group of researchers and practitioners who think about and engage with architecture and space through sound. We draw upon expertise both from within and beyond the field of architecture, inviting speakers with various academic, professional and practice backgrounds, including musicology, anthropology, computing, engineering, mathematics, performance studies, planning, and acoustics.
Details of our upcoming seminars and events can be found on our events tab.
